About Kiri

Hi, I'm Kiri. An artist based in Melbourne, originally from Japan.

I create hand-painted illustrations using acrylic gouache, with a focus on warm, heartfelt storytelling.

My work is inspired by everyday life, subtle cultural contrasts, and small moments of happiness. I love capturing those feelings through portrait commissions, narrative pieces, and creative workshops.

Each piece is painted by hand with care and intention. If you're looking to bring a personal story to life through art, I'd love to hear from you!
