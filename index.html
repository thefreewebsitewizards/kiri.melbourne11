About Kiri

Welcome to my creative world! I'm Kiri, a passionate artist based in Melbourne, specializing in custom portraits and handmade creations.

My artistic journey began with a love for capturing the essence of people and pets through detailed portraits. Each piece I create is infused with care, attention to detail, and a deep appreciation for the stories behind every commission.

My Mission

To create meaningful, personalized artwork that celebrates your most cherished memories and brings joy to your everyday life. Whether it's a custom portrait of your beloved pet, a family commission, or unique handmade accessories, every piece is crafted with love and attention to detail.

Artistic Style

My work blends realistic detail with warm, earthy tones that create a timeless and elegant feel. I believe in the power of art to tell stories and evoke emotions, which is why each commission is approached as a unique narrative waiting to be brought to life.
